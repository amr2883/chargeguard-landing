Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. What Data We Process

ChargeGuard processes the following limited data to detect and prevent carding attacks:

  • Customer IP address
  • Customer email address (hashed)
  • Order total value
  • A randomly generated device fingerprint

2. How We Use It

This data is exclusively used for fraud risk assessment. It is not shared with any third party, sold, or used for any other purpose.

3. Data Retention

Risk assessment data is retained for a maximum of 90 days to improve detection accuracy, then permanently deleted.

4. Contact

For questions: support@chargeguard.app
