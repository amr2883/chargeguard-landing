About ChargeGuard

ChargeGuard was born from a simple observation: WooCommerce store owners are losing thousands of dollars to automated card testing attacks, and existing security solutions weren't designed to stop them.

We are a team of security engineers and e-commerce specialists dedicated to one mission: protecting independent store owners from fraud before it costs them a single cent.

Our Promise

We believe every store owner deserves enterprise-grade security without the enterprise price tag. That's why we're launching with a free Early Access program for the first 10 stores.
